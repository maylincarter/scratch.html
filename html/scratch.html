Play Beach Runner!

Beach Runner is a vibrant, single-file HTML5 endless runner where players dash along a shoreline that dynamically shifts from morning sunlight to a starry night as they progress. Featuring responsive controls for both desktop and mobile, players must utilize a mix of jumping and sliding to dodge complex obstacles like bouncing beach balls and low-flying seagulls, while collecting sun hats for protection. The game sets itself apart with a unique "sonar" audio system that uses rising pitch cues to help players time their movements, creating an immersive, skill-based arcade experience that runs entirely in the browser without external downloads.
